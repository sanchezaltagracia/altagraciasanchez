The Domnican Republic

I am going to tell you about my native country, the beautiful Dominican Republic.
The Dominican Republic is a caribbean Nation that shares the island of Hispaniola with Haiti to the west.

The capital is Santo Domingo. Its known for its beaches, resorts, golfing.
These are the famous places; Sosua, Juan Dolio, Punta Cana, Puerto Plata, Las Terrenas de Samana, Barahona, and Boca Chica.

The typical music of the Dominican Republic is Merengue Ripiao, Merengue, and Bachata.

The Flag of the Dominican Republic

The flag of the Dominican Republic has a centered white cross that extends to the edges.
This emblem is similar to flag desing and shows a bibles across of gold and the dominican flags.
There are branches of olive and palm around the shield, and above on the ribbon, in the motto God, Country, Freedom.
The flag was designed by Juan Pablo Duarte, father of the Nation Independence of the Dominican Republic.
The natonal anthem of the Dominican Republic is courageous Dominicans "Quisqueyanos Valientes"
