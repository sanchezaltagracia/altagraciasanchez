Miami Beach, FL

This is a link to Miami Beach

Traveling gives me much joy by plane or by land,
traveling by plane gives me a lot of different sensations, of excitement and fear.
Especially if there is a lot of turbulence. Because it gives me a lot of fear, and nerves.
Once the plane hits the ground I feel safe, and it fills me with joy. Traveling on land can better enjoy nature and its landscapes.
You can stop when you want, drink coffee, eat, and rest.

On my visit to beautiful city of Miami Florida,
I had the opportunity to visit Miami Beach and one of the most famous boulevards, called Ocean Drive.
I was able to enjoy the beautiful colors and lights that decorate this lovely city.
The best way to enjoy Ocean Drive Boulevard is to walk down the street
which is characterized by its distinctive decorations of business art and its neon lights.
Which are loud and flashy.

Shops, Restaurants, and The Beach

This is a link to Mango's

There are many shops, clubs, restaurants along the way.
They are open day and night. The waiters are very polite and friendly,
they always have a smile on their face and they serve you with love. Mango's is a tropical cafe in south beach. It is an open bar serving quesadillas, fried chicken wings, fries, salads, alcohol and non-alcoholic drinks. and much more.
At night it becomes a place full of activities, artists who sing and dance live.
They get you on the stage to dance and sing with them.The truth is that you can enjoy your vacations to the fullest.
The truth is that I enjoy my vacations.

On the beach you can relax, tan, talk with other people,
enjoy water activities, such as swimming, surfing, jet skiing, snorkeling, and much more.
On the beach it is allowed to take topless sunbathing.
The sand slides by your fingers and feels very nice, there are also people who offer you massage at a considerable price.
I thoroughly enjoyed my stay here in Miami.
